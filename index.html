About NotThatTrip

NotThatTrip is a cutting-edge travel planning app designed specifically for iOS. Built with Swift and SwiftUI, it allows you to create detailed trips, organize them by day, and manage your destinations seamlessly. Whether you’re traveling by car or on foot, the integrated dynamic route planner with Apple Maps offers real-time travel times and intuitive visuals.

Features

Trip Planning

Create and organize trips effortlessly by dividing them into individual days.

Itinerary Management

Add, edit, and manage destinations with custom notes and selectable transportation modes.

Dynamic Mapping

Visualize your routes on Apple Maps with detailed travel time annotations for both driving and walking.

Adaptive UI

Enjoy a modern, sleek interface that automatically adjusts for light and dark modes.

User-Friendly Experience

Interactive elements and intuitive design make travel planning both fun and efficient.

Get Started

Clone the repository and open the project in Xcode to build and run the app on your iOS device or simulator. NotThatTrip requires iOS 16.6+ and Xcode 12+.
